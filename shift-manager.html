Shift Manager


Description

This project entails the development of an application designed to streamline employee management within the company. It offers a comprehensive solution by allowing the HR department to assign specific skills to employees and allocate shifts based on the HR manager's skill requirements. This application not only enhances workforce management but also ensures efficient staffing to meet the evolving needs of the organization.

Technologies

ReactJS, NodeJS, PostgreSQL, ExpressJS
