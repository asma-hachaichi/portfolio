Description

Introducing our Dental Practice Management Application tailored exclusively for pediatric dental clinics.
This comprehensive solution streamlines patient record management, supports various examination types, simplifies appointment scheduling, tracks patient histories, and facilitates payment summaries for dentists.
Designed with a child-friendly interface, it ensures a positive dental experience while optimizing administrative tasks, making it the ideal tool for dentists dedicated to providing top-quality care for young patients.

Technologies

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Ajax, MySQL
