Description

I had the opportunity to create a specialized website that plays a critical role in the efficient operation of a methanization station. This website is designed to monitor and supervise the various components of the station, providing real-time insights into their states and ensuring the smooth functioning of this environmentally significant process.
To enhance the safety and reliability of the methanization process, the website is equipped with an alarm system. It sends instant alerts in case of any anomalies or irregularities, allowing for swift corrective actions.
